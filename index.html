WHAT IS COMPUTER SECURITY?
Computer security refers to the mechanism of protecting and securing computers and their related data, networks, software, hardware from unauthorized access, misuse and theft.

TYPES OF COMPUTER SECURITY

(a)Cyber Security.
This means securing computers, electronic devices, networks, programs, systems from cyber attacks. Cyber attacks are those kind of attacks that happen when you are connected on the internet.
(b)Application security
This means securing the applications and data so that they are hacked. By doing this, the database application remains safe and private to the owner and users of the systems.

STRUCTURE OF A SECURITY POLICY

  • Description of the Policy and what is the usage for?
  • Where this policy should be applied?
  • Procedures that are involved in this policy.
  • Consequences if the policy is not compatible with company standards.
  • Functions and responsibilities of the employees that are affected by this policy.
